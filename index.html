About Coin Pop

Coin Pop is an engaging online earning game where you pop balloons to collect virtual coins. These coins can then be converted into real Pakistani Rupees (PKR) and withdrawn directly to your Jazzcash or Easypaisa accounts.

Our mission is to provide a fun, accessible, and rewarding experience for everyone. Start playing today and turn your taps into cash!

Our Vision

To be the leading casual gaming platform that empowers users to earn real money while enjoying simple, engaging games.

Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your choices regarding your data.

Information We Collect

  • **User Data:** Your chosen username (if applicable) and in-game progress (coin balance, game scores). This is stored locally on your device.
  • **Withdrawal Information (if submitted):** Amount, payment method (Jazzcash/Easypaisa), and account number provided by you for withdrawal purposes. This data is for processing your manual payout requests.
