Frequently Asked Questions

How long do withdrawals take?

EasyPaisa and JazzCash withdrawals are processed within 2-6 hours. Bank transfers may take 1-2 business days.

Why is my withdrawal pending?

Withdrawals are manually processed for security. Our team reviews each request to ensure everything is correct.

Can I change my payment method?

Yes, you can update your payment methods in the Settings page at any time.

Is there a minimum withdrawal amount?

Yes, the minimum withdrawal is PKR 250 (25,000 coins).
